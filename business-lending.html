Business Lending

We help you raise structured debt with minimum effort and without any hidden charges or collateral. A dedicated associate will ensure seamless execution of the entire process.

Get in Touch

We facilitate unsecured business loans in partnership with leading payment fintech firms and lenders including NBFCs and family across India enabling a wide variety of competitive debt options. We will support you in navigating the complexities of debt raising process. With our expertise in negotiation we ensure that you receive favourable terms and repayment option in alignment with your financial strength. The customized debt solutions include term loans, working capital limits, bill discounting and other similar ones. Every financial solution is structured to perfectly match your needs.

Our Comprehensive Business Lending Services

Identification of Lenders & Funding Options

Identification of suitable lenders and funding options, that serves as the foundational step in your business lending journey. We help you discover the right financial partners tailored to your business requirements.

Comprehensive Needs Assessment & Financial Analysis

Conducting a comprehensive needs assessment, perform detailed financial analysis, and evaluate creditworthiness to determine financial viability. We ensure a thorough evaluation of your financial position.

Strategic Debt Structuring & Optimization

Strategic structuring of debt requirements, by aligning financial needs, optimizing cost of capital, and ensuring repayments are tailored to the company's cash flow. We design debt solutions that work for you.

Lender Collaboration & Fund Disbursement

Collaborating closely with lenders to secure best terms, finalize legal agreements, and ensure seamless fund disbursement and minimizing administrative delays. We manage the complete execution process.
