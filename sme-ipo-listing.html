SME IPO Advisory Services

We specialize in facilitating strategic fundraising through SME IPO listings that enables businesses to attain their growth ambitions. SMEs are further provided with that vital access to pre-IPO funding customised to their needs before going public. A SME IPO offers numerous benefits like expanding operations, preparing for next-level of scaling, leading to increased market credibility, enhanced brand recognition, and driving the growth. With the requisite financial resources and strategic support system we turn their vision into reality.

Our Fundraising Support Includes

IPO Readiness & Eligibility Assessment

Comprehensive assessment of the SME to determine eligibility and preparedness for IPO listing.

Appointment of Intermediaries

Appointment of Merchant Banker (Lead Manager), Registrar, Legal Counsel, Auditors and other key intermediaries.

Exhaustive Due Diligence

In-depth financial, legal, and business due-diligence to ensure full regulatory compliance and clean records.

DRHP Preparation & Filing

Preparation and filing of Draft Red Herring Prospectus (DRHP) and all requisite SEBI & stock exchange documents.

SEBI & Exchange Query Resolution

Efficient handling of stock exchange and SEBI queries and securing in-principle and final approvals.

RHP Filing & Roadshows

Preparation and filing of Red Herring Prospectus (RHP) along with investor roadshows (physical & virtual).

Investor Engagement

Engaging QIBs, HNIs, retail and anchor investors through targeted meetings and marketing campaigns.

Seamless Issue Opening & Listing

Ensuring smooth subscription process, basis of allotment, and successful listing on BSE SME / NSE Emerge.

Post-IPO Compliance & Support

Long-term partnership for post-listing compliance, investor relations, corporate governance and sustained growth.
