Curriculum

What Will You Learn

Five power-packed modules designed to give you clarity, control, and the confidence to scale your business with financial intelligence.

01
Financial Foundation
  • Bookkeeping system vs. a financial operating system
  • Three questions your financial system must answer — right now
  • Where are we? Where are we going? What next?
02
Cash Flow Architecture
  • Identifying cash conversion cycles in your business model
  • Map your own cycle: from expense to revenue receipt
  • Plugging cash leaks before they become crises
03
Growth Metrics & KPIs
  • Vanity metrics vs. actionable financial metrics
  • Unit economics — anchor every decision in unit profitability
  • Building a KPI dashboard for your business model
04
Financial Planning & Scenario Modelling
  • Annual budgeting vs. rolling forecasts — which to use when
  • Decision gates: financial triggers to accelerate, hold, or cut
  • 12-month financial model you'll build live
05
Systems, Tools & Automation
  • Financial tech stack — tools that save hours every week
  • When and how to hire a CFO / Fractional CFO
  • Tech stack gap analysis for your stage
